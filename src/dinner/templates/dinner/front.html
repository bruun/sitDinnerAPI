SiT Kafe API

FAQ

  1. How do I use the API?

    There are several ways to access the data:

    • List cafeterias: http://sit.tbruun.no/api/cafeterias/
    • Meal for day in cafeteria: http://sit.tbruun.no/api/cafeteria/NAVN/YYYY-MM-DD
    • Meal from day in week: http://sit.tbruun.no/api/cafeteria/NAVN/weekly/YYYY-MM-DD

  2. Where do you get the data from?

    All menus are found here: http://sit.no/content/31542/Middag

  3. Are you affiliated with SiT?

    This API is developed independently of SiT.
    {% comment %}The iPhone app was developed independently of SiT, and published in cooperation with SiT

    {% endcomment %}

  4. Is this API free?

    This API is free for non-commercial use.

  5. The API says no meal was found on a given weekday, but the SiT website claims there is.

    The SiT RSS-feeds change from time to time, making this site unable to process them.
    If you discover such a case, please send me an email at thomas(dot)bruun(at)gmail(dot)com, with information about the cafeteria and date.

  6. Can I access menus from previous weeks?

    Yes, if it was accessed via the API that week. Unfortunately SiT does not keep a record of meals from previous weeks.

  7. I am having problems with the iPhone app.

    I the app claims that no meal was found, please refer to question 4.
    For other issues, please send me an email at thomas(dot)bruun(at)gmail(dot)com, with information with your iPhone model, iOS version, and an explanation of the issue

  8. Who are you?

    My name is Thomas Bruun, and I study computer science at the Norwegian University of Science and Technology (NUTS!).

  9. What is the API written in?

    Django! <3

  10. What does the future hold?

    My goal is to parse the flags for vegetarian (V), lactose reduced (L) and gluten free (G) food into separate fields.
    In the iPhone app this could mean separate icons for each of these properties.
    Furthermore, I want to add support for cafeterias with lunch menus, such as 'EL-kantinen'.

    If you have any other ideas of improvement, don't hesitate to contact me!
